
Here are some essential modules for a School Management System to effectively manage various aspects of a school:

1. Student Management
   - Admission and enrollment
   - Student profiles (personal details, academic records, attendance, etc.)
   - Class and section allocation
   - ID card generation

2. Teacher and Staff Management
   - Teacher profiles (qualifications, schedules, subjects)
   - Staff roles and responsibilities
   - Attendance and payroll

3. Attendance Management
   - Student and staff attendance tracking
   - Integration with biometric or RFID systems
   - Daily, weekly, and monthly attendance reports

4. Fee Management
   - Fee structure setup
   - Online fee collection
   - Fee receipts and overdue alerts
   - Scholarships and concessions

5. Administrative Management
   - User roles and permissions
